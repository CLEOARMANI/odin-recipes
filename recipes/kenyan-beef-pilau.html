Kenyan Beef Pilau

Description

Pilau has a wonderful balance of flavors. It is a festive dish, which is never missing during special occasions or events. It’s made with rice cooked in a well-seasoned broth of Meat or chicken. Unlike the Indian pilau, the East African version does not use curry and it’s less spicy.

ingredients

  • 1/2kg Cubed beef
  • 2 Cups rice
  • 2 Garlic bulbs minced
  • 4 Large onions,finely chopped
  • Whole pilau spice(a blend of black pepper, cumin seeds, cardamom, cloves and cinnamon)
  • Tomato paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Vegetable oil
  • Garlic and ginger paste
  • 5 Cubed potatoes

Directions:

    Step 1:

    Boil the meat until its tender

    Step 2:

    In a coocking pot Heat the oil and add the onions cook until golden brown

    Step 3:

    Add the garlic paste followed by the ginger paste and cook for 2 mins

    Step 4:

    Add the grounded pilau masala and the cumin, cardamon and cinnamon cook for a minute

    Step 5:

    Add the boiled meat and potatoes and cook for 2 mins

    Step 6:

    Add the rice and 4 cups of water, stir and let it boil

    Step 7:

    When the water reduces cover the Cooking pot with a lid and reduce the flame to low for 5-10 minutes and your piau is ready!

Serving suggestions

Best served with pilipili ya kukaanga, kachumbari and any juice of your liking.Enjoy!
